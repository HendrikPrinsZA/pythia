## Example
```
> python3 pythia.py

Instructions: capture weight and height to calculate bmi
```
Generated solution
```py
#!/usr/bin/env python3

weight = float(input("What is your weight in kg? "))
height = float(input("What is your height in m? "))

bmi = weight / (height * height)

print("Your BMI is: " + str(bmi))
```

## Who was Pythia?
Pythia was the name of the high priestess of the Temple of Apollo at Delphi. She specifically served as its oracle and was known as the Oracle of Delphi. Her title was also historically glossed in English as the Pythoness. The name Pythia is derived from Pytho, which in myth was the original name of Delphi. - https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pythia
